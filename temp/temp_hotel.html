Stay in the heart of Lisbon

The hotel is located in the historical centre of the city, immersed in the rich culture and traditions, for which it is renowned. Guests can explore the charming district of Rossio, or venture a little further and discover the beauty of Alfama, Chiado and Bairro Alto.

This hotel is just a 15 minutes' walk from Sao Jorge Castle. Rua Augusta shopping street can be found just 400 m, where guests can indulge in a spot of retail therapy.

Lisbon Airport is just a short drive away. This charming hotel dates back to the early 20th century.

The guest rooms were stunningly designed and equipped with air-conditioning, flat-screen TV and private bathroom with toiletries. The hotel's extensive range of facilities and services is sure to impress even the most discerning traveller.
