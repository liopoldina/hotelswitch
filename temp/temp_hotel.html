Stay in the heart of Lisbon

This hotel is located in a privileged area of Lisbon's city centre, near Marquês de Pombal, Park Edward VII and the Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation. The hotel is only 4 metro stations from the historic city centre of Baixa-Chiado, Bairro Alto, Rossio and Restauradores.

The hotel is also close Avenida da Liberdade and the El Corte Ingles department store. The São Sebastião metro station (Blue and Red lines) is opposite the hotel.
